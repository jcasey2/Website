Hello! My name is John Casey and I am 29 years old, married and have one daughter born this year. I am an undergraduate Computer Science student at the University of South Florida in my last semester. Originally I am from the Boston area, Lynn specifically, but I have been in Florida for 27+ years so I am basically a Floridian now. I am however a Boston sports fan, mostly Red Sox and Patriots, and to some extent the Bruins, and a passing interest in the Celtics. Right now we live in Ruskin, FL for the moment until I graduate and find a job either here or preferably somewhere else. I may eat those words depending on where we end up.

Why computer science? It is a trio of what I think people should go to college for. It is something that interests me, it is something that challenges me academically, and it is something that I can make a decent living at. It took me awhile to find out what I wanted to do. I am 29 after all, not exactly typical college age. The first round in college did not go so well mostly because I did not know what I wanted to do for the next forty years. I think that is a lot to ask an eighteen year old adult. Especially one who is not lucky enough to have not found something that they are passionate about yet.

My hobbies do not really correlate with each other nor do they only pertain to technical areas. I like to think that I am a jack of all trades but master of none. I enjoy anything from astronomy to carpentry. A short list of my favorite hobbies are: astronomy, photography, aviation, electronics, web development, video games, carpentry. The list really could go on and on.

I started this website as a way to stand out among the other computer science students vying for jobs. Still in it's infancy, this website plans on housing more about myself and my family as well as my professional career. Right now though it is just another hobby of mine that I work on in my free time while I have nothing else to do. Although it may have a primitive look to it now, I plan on making it look better as I get better at making a website.

That is about it. If you would like to send an email to me feel free to visit the contact page and send an email. Write anything you would like (or don't like) about the website, me, or anything you want. Well maybe not anything. Don't be mean and we should be good. I do not expect much traffic here so anything is welcome. Other than that check out the rest of the site.
